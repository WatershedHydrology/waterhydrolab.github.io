We develop data-driven modeling frameworks and decision-support tools
that improve water quality, resource management, and sustainability in
working agricultural systems. We integrate field observations,
geospatial data, and process-based models to better understand how
management practices influence environmental and economic outcomes.

### What we build

- Watershed-scale modeling and analysis pipelines that harmonize land
  use, soils, weather, and management data.
- Decision-support systems (DSS) that combine modeling outputs with
  field-based information to guide management.
- Approaches for pasture and biomass assessment combining field
  measurements with data-driven and imagery-based methods.

### Stakeholder engagement

We work directly with **ranchers, Extension agents, and stakeholders**
to make sure the tools support practical decision-making in real-world
operations. Our Extension appointment is a 40% component of Dr.
Golmohammadi's position.
